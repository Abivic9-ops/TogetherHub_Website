Our Growth Journey

Milestones and achievements that shaped TogetherHub

2020

Platform Launch

TogetherHub launches with vision to connect communities and drive social change. 100 founding members across 5 regions.

2021

Community Expansion

Scaled to 1,200+ volunteers across 15 communities. Reached 15,000+ people through 42 completed projects.

2022

Impact Milestone

Hit 4,500+ volunteers and 150+ completed projects. Impacted 85,000+ lives. Expanded to 28 countries globally.

Global Presence

2025-2026

Future Vision
