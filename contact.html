Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I join TogetherHub?

Joining is simple! Click the "Join the Movement" button anywhere on our site, fill out your profile with your name, email, and location, and you're ready to start. It takes less than two minutes.

Is TogetherHub free to use?

Yes! TogetherHub is completely free for all community members. We believe social impact should be accessible to everyone, regardless of financial means.

How are projects funded?

Projects are funded through community contributions, grants, partnerships, and crowdfunding campaigns. We provide tools and support to help communities raise the resources they need.

Can I volunteer if I don't have specific skills?

Absolutely! Every community project needs diverse skills and perspectives. Whether you can donate time, skills, or just enthusiasm, there's a role for you.

How does TogetherHub measure impact?

We track multiple metrics: lives directly impacted, projects completed, funding mobilized, and long-term community outcomes. Every project shares real data about its progress and results.

Can organizations partner with TogetherHub?

Yes! We welcome partnerships with NGOs, businesses, educational institutions, and government bodies. Contact our partnerships team at partner@togetherhub.org to explore collaboration.

Is my data secure on TogetherHub?

Data security is paramount. We use industry-standard encryption, comply with GDPR and international privacy regulations, and never share your information without consent.

How can I start a community project?

Log into your account, navigate to "Start a Project," tell us about your idea, identify impact metrics, and launch. Our team provides resources and support throughout.
